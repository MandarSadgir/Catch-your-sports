USA vs IRE Dream11 Prediction, Fantasy Cricket Tips, Playing 11, Pitch Report and Injury Update for 2nd ODI

Preview:


United States of America will take on Ireland in the 2nd ODI of the series on Tuesday. The 1st ODI was abandoned as the match officials tested positive for Covid-19. The remaining two ODI will go ahead as per the schedule. Both sides managed to level the T20I series 1-1 and it brings more focus towards the ODI series. The USA last played an ODI in the ICC Men’s Cricket World Cup League 2 and sit at the third spot in the standings with 14 points.


Ireland have plenty of experience and has done a commendable job against the top-quality sides in the past. They have drawn their last 2 previous series 1-1 against Ireland and South Africa. Considering their experiences at the international level, Ireland will start as favorites for this match.
